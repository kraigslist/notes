## Diligence Questions

- Confirm exact list price and whether seller concessions are available.
- Confirm exact loan balance, payment, servicer, and remaining term.
- Confirm whether the loan is VA and whether entitlement substitution/release is available.
- Confirm whether the seller has already started the assumption package.
- Ask for servicer timeline and assumption requirements.
- Confirm all assumption fees, VA funding fee treatment, closing costs, and whether there are any second liens.
- Confirm the full HOA amount and what it includes.
- Review HOA reserves, budget, meeting minutes, insurance master policy, litigation, special assessments, rental restrictions, and maintenance responsibilities.
- Confirm whether the HOA has any upcoming major exterior, roof, siding, paving, or insurance cost increases.
- Confirm property tax amount and whether taxes are likely to reset or change materially after sale.
- Ask for recent inspection/repair history, including roof, exterior envelope, windows, HVAC, plumbing, and any water intrusion issues.
