## How This Location Fits the Buy Decision

**Strengths**
- West-side suburban lifestyle: quiet streets, parks/trails, strong regional schools (verify boundaries)
- Proximity to Intel/Nike/tech corridor and Tanasbourne employment
- Affluent, stable owner-occupier area (Rock Creek / west 97229) with long-term demand fundamentals
- THPRD parks/trails add outdoor access without HOA (this house has no HOA)

**Tradeoffs**
- Car-dependent; weak walkability/transit vs. inner Portland
- 1987 build + crawl space → higher inspection/m maintenance risk than newer Bethany inventory
- Hillsboro SD vs. Beaverton SD may affect resale audience for some buyers
- Short-hold risk if values flatten: transaction costs eat equity quickly (see house notes)

**Vs. Peregrine list price:** You're buying **at or slightly above** Rock Creek medians but **at or slightly below** the agent's sold-comp average. The assumable VA loan is the main economic edge; neighborhood fundamentals support a **5–7+ year hold** more than a 2–3 year flip.
