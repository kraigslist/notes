## Overall impression

The house presents as **clean, bright, and move-in ready**. Staging does its job: neutral gray paint, good natural light, vaulted ceilings, and an open sunken living room give it a spacious feel for ~2,332 sq ft. For a **1987 Rock Creek build**, it looks **well cared for cosmetically**, not fully renovated.

The real economic win is still the loan: **3.49% on ~$628k** with only ~**$51k equity gap**. The photos don't change that math — they help decide whether this is a house worth owning for 7–10+ years, not just a rate to capture.

**Verdict:** Legitimate assumable-loan opportunity on a house that looks livable and pleasant, not a turnkey renovated comp. Seller refreshed paint/staging and maintains the interior well while **deferring exterior/deck work** that cedar-sided 1987 houses eventually need. Price in **$5–15k near-term capex** in offer math; use inspection to negotiate.

## Room-by-room inspection checklist

Use during walkthrough and with your inspector. Check `[ ]` items on site; note findings inline.

**Deal-breaker threshold (from offer strategy):** terminate or renegotiate if near-term capex exceeds **~$25k** (roof + siding + sewer combined).

### Exterior — front

**Photos:** `01`–`03`

- [ ] **Siding material** — confirm cedar vs. fiber-cement; note stain/paint age and flaking
- [ ] **Siding condition** — soft spots, rot at corners, trim, window sills, chimney chase
- [ ] **Roof (ground view)** — missing/damaged shingles, moss, sagging ridges, age estimate
- [ ] **Gutters/downspouts** — attachment, rust, overflow staining on siding
- [ ] **Front porch/deck steps** — rot, loose boards, railing stability, peeling stain
- [ ] **Driveway** — cracks, settling, drainage toward foundation or garage

## Walk-away triggers (recap)

Terminate or hard renegotiate if:

- Servicer won't allow entitlement substitution / seller release

- Second lien, solar lease, or title issue blocks assumption
- Inspection finds **$25k+** near-term capex (roof + siding + sewer)
- Seller won't grant **60+ day** close for assumption processing
- Crawl space shows active moisture or structural concern
- Sewer scope shows major failure requiring replacement
