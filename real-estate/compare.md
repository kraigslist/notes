## Tradeoff Summary

- **Beaverton:** Best monthly affordability and lowest price, but ~$425/mo HOA and condo/townhouse diligence. Equity gap ~$100k — middle of the pack.
- **Chemeketa:** Excellent rate and sub-$3k monthly, but ~$189k equity gap is the defining constraint. Best monthly-budget detached option if cash is not the bottleneck.
- **Peregrine:** Highest monthly cost (**$3,637.57 confirmed**), but smallest equity gap (**~$51k confirmed**) and loan balance nearly equals price — easiest assumable close. Best if upfront cash matters more than monthly payment.

## Cross-Cutting Diligence

Applies to all three listings:

- Confirm exact loan balance, payment, servicer, and remaining term
- Confirm VA loan status and entitlement substitution/release for seller
- Ask whether assumption package has already been started; get servicer timeline
- Confirm assumption fees, VA funding fee treatment, and full closing cost estimate
- Rule out second liens, solar loans, assessments, or title issues
- Get current loan statement from seller or listing agent
