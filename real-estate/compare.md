## Cross-Cutting Diligence

Applies to all listings:

- Confirm exact loan balance, payment, servicer, and remaining term
- Confirm loan type (FHA, VA, USDA, or other) and assumption eligibility
- For VA: confirm entitlement substitution and seller liability release
- Ask whether assumption package has already been started; get servicer timeline (typically 2–4 months)
- Confirm assumption fees, funding fee treatment, and full closing cost estimate
- Rule out second liens, solar UCC filings, assessments, or title issues
- Get current loan statement from seller or listing agent
- For townhouses/condos: HOA reserves, assessments, master insurance, rental caps
